The Usage Of Sevoflurane

For what Sevoflurane?

Sevoflurane is Anastesy. It works by suppressing activity in the central nervous system, causing loss of consciousness. Cause general anesthesia or anaesthesia total (loss of consciousness) prior to and during operation.

How to Use Sevoflurane?

Use Sevoflurane as directed by your doctor. Check the label on the medicine for exact dosing instructions. Sevoflurane is usually given by a doctor or other health care provider during the operation. If you miss a dose of Sevoflurane, immediately contact your doctor.
